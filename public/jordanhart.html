Video Marketing Case: Jordan Hart

Title:

"Whole Lot of Love"

Location:

Vitaly Compound, Queen W.

Work:

Filming, Editing, Motion

Tools:

Canon C100 Mk ii, Lumix GH5-S, Photoshop, Illustrator, Premiere, After Effects

The Story

Jordan Hart’s voice and talent are incredible. While many emerging artists use busking as a practice tool, Jordan was clearly a seasoned professional. Upon hearing his voice, I asked if I could film him busking and he openly agreed. A few days later, we met to discuss how we could use film and social media to add to Jordan’s rising popularity in the city. We agreed on the “100 day busking challenge,” where rain or shine, Jordan would sing his heart out, and we’d capture the process.

The production workflow of travelling with 3 cameras + stabilizers and tripods, inconsistent lighting, hour-long sessions, editing, and uploading the daily videos to the world was simply too much work for one person. My partner helped to establish “We The Iconic,” our label for carrying out the process.

On Day 18, Jordan was interviewed by Michelle Dube, CTV’s Best News Anchor (award), for a segment that would go on live news across Toronto.

Results

By the end of the 100 day busking challenge, Jordan was in negotiations with multiple producers and labels.
