Commercial Case: Explainer Video (Pharmaceutical)

Brief:

Delivery of an explainer video to help TwoLabs’ clients learn more about the multitude of services offered

Responsibilities:

Direction (collaborative), voice acting, audio mastering, compositing, delivery

Tools:

Photoshop, Illustrator, Audition, Premiere, After Effects

The Story

I was contracted to create an explainer video in a fast-paced and tightly budgeted environment. The art style was loosely provided and I was expected to create further assets based on initial storyboards and visuals.

Storyboarding, Voice Recording, and Leica

While the script had been pre-arranged by the clients, the storyboard had not yet been flushed out. I immediately voice-recorded and created a leica to ensure visuals, vocal pacing, and preliminary music choices were approved. Compositing this project was extremely smooth, with the greatest challenge lying in the preparation of AI assets for use in AE. This project utilized an AE camera to pan across areas of the underlying "map" to enhance the concept of a journey from start to end. While the camera operated in a 3D space, keeping all assets 2D made the process much faster than opting for full 3D.
