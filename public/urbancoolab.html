The Story

Urbancoolab is a ground-breaking startup that aims to help influencers create their own fashion brands in as few steps as possible. Idris is not only the co-founder of Urbancoolab but has multiple successful start-up exits under his belt most notably Idea Couture. It was a pleasure for me to help direct, film, and composite a video series that effectively represented Urbancoolab’s brand and targeted their audience.

Motion Design Process

Shoot Day:

With a tight budget, limited location options, and just a single day to capture all the footage, organization was of utmost importance. Prior to the shoot day, I extensively tested lighting, audio, and power conditions in the chosen set location. On shoot day, there was also a last-minute addition of a photoshoot which would normally be a challenge for the talent. Luckily, we were working with @wizthemc, whose limitless energy kept the shoot fun, expressive, and lively.

Color Grading & Music

Idris’ team included a music producer whose tracks represented the streetwear vibes that needed to be present in the videos. Deciding on which tracks to use for the different videos required deep thought about the mood of each video’s story.

Color grading for this project was a huge challenge. The key light I had extensively tested happened to blow out just minutes before our first take. Since we were color matching LOG footage across two different LOG types (cLog-2 and vLog-L), the lighting had to be carefully reset. To ensure that all the shots (cam a + cam b + cam c) looked similar while not clipping blacks or highlights, I set up a 7,680px * 2,160px composition in After Effects and overlayed a gigantic de-noise filter and color correction layer over all the different shot types.

Motion Assets

UI Elements:

The design of the motion assets were entirely under my creative control except for the broad color scheme. The assets were a pleasure to make, and I was ecstatic to utilize AE 2020’s new shape-group features as well as an AI-to-AE plugin called "Overlord" to speed up the workflow.
